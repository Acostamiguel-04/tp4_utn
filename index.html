Londres

On this walk we will visit the district of Westminster,
a neighborhood in central London north of the Thames.
We will see such famous monuments as Buckingham Palace,
the official residence of Queen Elizabeth II,
the Wellington Barracks and the famous Changing of the Guard

Toronto

Toronto is a dynamic and cosmopolitan city,
and one of Canada's most popular destinations.
It is a nerve center of culture, music and gastronomy,
to which must be added the interesting diversity of its neighbourhoods,
arranged around the old town.
